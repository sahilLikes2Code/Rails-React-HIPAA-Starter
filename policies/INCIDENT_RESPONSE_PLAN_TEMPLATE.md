# HIPAA Incident Response Plan Template

**IMPORTANT:** This is a template. Customize it for your organization and have it reviewed by legal counsel before use.

## [Your Organization Name] HIPAA Breach Incident Response Plan

**Effective Date:** [Date]

### 1. Purpose

This plan outlines procedures for responding to potential HIPAA breaches and security incidents.

### 2. Incident Classification

- **Breach**: Unauthorized access, use, or disclosure of PHI
- **Security Incident**: Attempted or successful unauthorized access
- **Privacy Incident**: Violation of privacy policies

### 3. Response Procedures

#### Step 1: Detection and Containment
- Identify the incident
- Contain the breach immediately
- Preserve evidence

#### Step 2: Assessment
- Determine scope and impact
- Assess risk to PHI
- Document findings

#### Step 3: Notification
- Notify Security Officer within [X] hours
- Notify affected individuals within 60 days (if breach confirmed)
- Notify HHS within 60 days (if 500+ individuals affected)
- Notify media (if 500+ individuals affected)

#### Step 4: Mitigation
- Implement corrective actions
- Prevent future occurrences
- Update security measures

#### Step 5: Documentation
- Document all steps taken
- Maintain incident log
- Review and update policies
